What is this?

Just a random font generator for your designs.

Made to boost your creativity & for fun

  • Gives ideas

    In the process of creating a design, you can come up with completely unexpected ideas and concepts that you might not even have guessed before.

  • Kills boredom

    Tired of making boring and similar designs? Do you want to challenge yourself and try to do something unusual and new?
    You are in the right place.

  • Develops imagination

    If you often do something non-standard, and develop your imagination, then coming up with something later will not be a problem for you.

Tell me the story

The idea for this site came to me one summer day when I was taking a design course. We went through the topic "Typography", and the homework was to make the main screen of the site. It seemed a little boring to me, because there are a lot of fonts in the world, and it would be interesting to just experiment with some unusual fonts to learn how to feel them and work cool with typography.

And then I thought:

What if I make a site that will randomly select some unusual font, and then you need to make something using it?

The idea seemed very cool to me (well, still), plus - I wanted to make a modern site in the style of brutalism.

So here I am, making this BREATHTAKING design and writing this “funny” text haha.
